Services

Here is a list of the services that we can provide at MPW. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.

Automatic Gate Servicing

Call us the AA of gates. Regular servicing on your gates can really help to keep them in a good condition ensuring that they are working for you and that any issues are hopefully spotted at service stage which allows the engineer and yourself to discuss options.

Gate Installation

Looking to put gates on your property/commercial premises, or even upgrade an existing system or automate an existing gate? There are options to discuss. Again all options are bespoke to each customer and job type to ensure you get what is viable and will work best for you.

CCTV

Fitting cameras to your premises, home or commercial, can really help give peace of mind with the ability to check the cameras from within the property or when you are out and about or travelling.

Alarm Systems

Having an alarm fitted and serviced by an alarm engineer will support your peace of mind and also reduces home insurance premiums. Find out more today by reaching out to us.

Lighting

Lighting in the garden has the capability to look fantastic as well as provide a deterrent for unwanted visitors and also on a simpler level- the ability to find your keys when you arrive home in the dark!
