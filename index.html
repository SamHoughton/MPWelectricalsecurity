What We Offer

Everything Your Property Needs to Stay Secure

From a single camera to a fully integrated security system, every job is handled with the same level of care and attention to detail. Get a free quote today.

Automatic Gate Servicing

Think of us as the AA for your gates. Regular servicing keeps your automation running reliably, catches problems before they become costly, and extends the life of your system.

Gate Installation

Sliding, swing, bi-folding, timber or steel — whatever gate system suits your property, we design and install it from scratch. Every job is completely bespoke, no two the same.

CCTV Installation

Full HD cameras with night vision, motion alerts, and remote smartphone viewing. Know exactly what's happening at your property at any time, from anywhere in the world.

Alarm Systems

A professionally installed alarm is one of the most effective deterrents against break-ins — and could significantly reduce your home insurance premium. We install and service them all.

Security Lighting

Strategically placed driveway and garden lighting enhances your kerb appeal while acting as a powerful deterrent. From subtle pathway lights to full perimeter flood systems.
